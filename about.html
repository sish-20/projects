About MoneyMonitor

MoneyMonitor is a modern and intuitive web application designed to help users take full control of their personal finances. Built using cutting-edge technologies such as HTML, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, MoneyMonitor offers a seamless and responsive interface, ensuring that managing your expenses has never been easier. All data is securely stored locally in your browser using localStorage, providing both privacy and persistent access to your financial records without requiring a backend server.

Key Features

  • Intuitive user profiles for personalized financial management.
  • Effortlessly add, view, and delete income and expense transactions.
  • Dynamic updates to total income, total expenses, and net income.
  • Proactive warnings:
    • "Nearing money overuse" when expenses are high and net income falls below 1000.
    • "In debt" when net income reaches 0 or less.
  • Responsive design that adapts to any device.
  • Secure local data storage using localStorage.
